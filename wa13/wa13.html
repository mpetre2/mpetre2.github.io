Privacy Notice

This site does not collect, store, or share any personal information on a server. The only data saved is your selected news topics, which are stored locally in your browser so the site can remember your preferences. This information never leaves your device and is not transmitted, tracked, or shared with any third party. When news is loaded, only the chosen categories are sent to the New York Times API. No identifying information is included. You may clear your saved topics at any time, and they will be permanently removed from your browser.
