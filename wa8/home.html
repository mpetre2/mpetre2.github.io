    Privacy & Data

    We keep data collection minimal and transparent.

    What We Save

    • Theme preference (light/dark) so pages look the same next visit.
    • Hours you log (on your device only).
    • Saved events (on your device only).

    Where & How Long

    Stored in your browser’s Local Storage. Theme expires after 30 days; you can clear everything anytime.

    We don’t fetch external data; everything stays local to your browser.
